# LearnX AI
# KJU hackathon

Problem Statement: 
   
                 Traditional education systems fail to adapt to individual student learning needs, causing poor engagement and outcomes.
                 Traditional classroom and online learning methods rely heavily on one-size-fits-all content delivery, resulting in low student engagement, poor knowledge retention, and limited personalization to individual learning needs and pace.
                 
                 
                 
                 
Proposed Solution:

                An AI-powered platform that analyzes student performance and provides personalized learning paths.
                Provides a clear and clarity way for all category of  students to make their Learning Carrier more effective . 
                
                

Product Description:

               LearnX AI tracks learning behavior, recommends content, and adapts difficulty for better learning outcomes.
               Students will be having a assessment test.  Where our AI will identifies the strength and weakness of the student.
               So according to the analytics our AI will adapt the recommendations automatically.
